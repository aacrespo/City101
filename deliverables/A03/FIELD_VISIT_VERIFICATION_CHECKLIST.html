Cross-site patterns — reflect after all visits

Scale of the gap
Is 0.7km (Nyon) different from 1.4km (Morges) different from 2.1km (Rennaz)? At what distance does the gap become unacceptable?
Night evidence
Any signs of night activity? 24h signage, lit windows, security cameras, vending machines, worn paths?
The threshold
At each site, where exactly does "connected" become "disconnected"? A road? A hill? A fence? A last streetlight?
Who's present?
Even in afternoon visits: nurses in scrubs? Elderly with walkers? Delivery trucks? Nobody?
Public space
Is there ANY public space at or near each site? A bench? A café? A bus shelter? Or truly nothing?
Car dominance
How big are hospital parking lots? How many cars? More parking spaces than bus stops?
Proto-lock exists?
Is there already an informal "lock"? A bus shelter used as rest, a café where workers wait, a hospital lobby functioning as transit waiting room?
What data missed
Anything not in the JSX or datasets. New connections. Surprises. This is the most important row.
