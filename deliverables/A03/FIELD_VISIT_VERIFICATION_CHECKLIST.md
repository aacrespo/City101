## Cross-site patterns to watch for

After visiting all 7 sites, reflect on these:

| Pattern | What to look for across sites |
|---------|-------------------------------|
| **Scale of the gap** | Is 0.7km (Nyon) different from 1.4km (Morges) different from 2.1km (Rennaz)? At what distance does the gap become unacceptable? |
| **Night evidence** | Any signs of night activity? 24h signage, lit windows, security cameras, vending machines, worn paths? |
| **The threshold** | At each site, where exactly does "connected" become "disconnected"? Is it a road? A hill? A fence? A last streetlight? |
| **Who's present at 14:00–17:00?** | Even in afternoon visits, who do you see? Nurses in scrubs? Elderly with walkers? Delivery trucks? Nobody? |
| **Public space** | Is there ANY public space at or near each hospital/logistics site? A bench? A café? A bus shelter? Or truly nothing? |
| **Car dominance** | How big are hospital parking lots? How many cars? Are there more parking spaces than bus stops? |
| **The chamber already exists?** | Is there already an informal "lock" — a bus shelter being used as a rest spot, a café where night workers wait, a hospital lobby functioning as a waiting room for transit? |
| **What the data missed** | Anything you see that wasn't in the JSX files or datasets. New connections. Surprises. |
