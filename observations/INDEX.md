# Observations Index

Findings discovered through analysis. Status markers: **confirmed**, **needs verification**, **supporting evidence only**.

---

## 1. The Archipelago Pattern — confirmed
Only 11–14 of 49 stations maintain working continuity across time periods. Three structural gaps:
- Nyon → Gland (19.3 km)
- Gland → Morges (20 km)
- Lavaux Fracture (17.5 km)

The linear city doesn't exist. It's an archipelago.

## 2. Two Corridors on the Same Tracks — confirmed
IC passengers get WiFi, tables, quiet car — a workable linear city. S-Bahn passengers get doors every 90 seconds, no WiFi. ~85% of connections score as not workable. Same infrastructure, two completely different urban experiences.

## 3. Different City Per Person — confirmed
- GA holders: 101 km as one city (CHF 10.50/day amortized)
- Non-GA holders: fragmentation (CHF 26 Geneva → Lausanne)
- Frontaliers (~100k in Geneva canton): may face barriers to GA purchase — **practical uptake needs verification**

## 4. Diversity Creates Unity — confirmed (quadruple-validated)
Religious Shannon, modal Shannon, cuisine Shannon, and economic category diversity ALL converge on the same pattern (r = 0.63–0.71). Phase transition at Shannon ≈ 1.0: below it, avg richness 8.8; above, 70.5 (8x jump). Four independent indices prove the Lavaux Fracture is a systemic diversity collapse, not just a transit gap.

Two anomaly sites identified:
- Vernier-Blandonnet: diversity without programming
- Coppet: programming without diversity

## 5. 160,000 Ghost Citizens — confirmed
Combined GE + VD frontaliers exceed Lausanne's population. Present in daytime economy, invisible in every diversity metric, can't buy GA, vanish at 18:00. The corridor's diversity is time-dependent.

## 6. Structural, Not Temporal — confirmed
The number of workable stations barely changes across 7 time slots (14 at peak, 13 at 11pm). Gaps persist at all hours. The archipelago pattern is structural.

## 7. 42-Fold Frequency Variation — confirmed
Lausanne 28.5 trains/hr → St-Saphorin 0.0. Service windows range from 21.3h (Geneva) to 0h (St-Saphorin). This 42x variation is THE spatial argument for working continuity.

## 8. EV Charging — supporting evidence only
Confirms Lavaux gap from a third angle and demonstrates dwell-time-as-design-opportunity. Has dropped in relevance — working continuity narrative is about the universal commuter, not EV drivers specifically.

## 9. Commuter Index Classification — confirmed
SBB Passagierfrequenz workday/nonworkday ratio classifies stations:
- Lancy-Pont-Rouge CI=4.04 (pure commuter, dead weekends)
- Montreux CI=1.02 (balanced year-round)
- Épesses CI=0.47 (wine tourism, busier weekends)
