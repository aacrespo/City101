## 1. MEDICATION / PHARMA LOGISTICS

### 1.1 Wholesale Distribution Structure

Switzerland's pharma supply chain flows: **Manufacturer -> Pre-wholesaler (Alloga) -> Wholesaler (Galexis/Voigt) -> Hospital/Pharmacy/Doctor**.

**Galexis AG** (Galenica Group subsidiary) is Switzerland's largest healthcare wholesaler, supplying **two-thirds of all pharmacies** in Switzerland plus doctors, hospitals, and care homes. Moves **over 100 million packs per year** and can process **150,000 order lines per day**.
[SOURCE: https://www.galenica.com/en/news/entries/2022-09-07_avance_news.php]
[SOURCE: https://www.galenica.com/en/galenica-netzwerk/]

**Galexis Ecublens Distribution Center** (the corridor's pharma hub):
- **Address**: Route de Crochy 2, 1024 Ecublens (near Lausanne)
- Coordinates: 46.5350, 6.5703
- **Second-largest Galexis site**, hub for all of French-speaking Switzerland
- Ships up to **100,000 packs per day**
- CHF 34 million modernization completed 2022: roof raised 2.8m, 10,000 m3 hall, new automated order-picking system
- Operating hours: Mon-Fri 07:00-19:00, Sat 09:00-17:00
- Operating since the early 1970s
- **Corridor position**: ~km 60, directly adjacent to the Crissier-Bussigny Night Belt (Node 4)
[SOURCE: https://www.galenica.com/en/news/entries/2022-09-07_avance_news.php]
[SOURCE: https://www.local.ch/en/d/ecublens-vd/1024/wholesale-dealer/galexis-sa-CXIMgvi0e6DpqZ9N8MKJ1g]

**Alloga AG** (Galenica Group subsidiary, pre-wholesaler):
- **Headquarters**: Burgdorf (canton of Bern) -- not on corridor but feeds into it
- Almost **every second medication pack** provided to patients in Switzerland was once stored at Alloga
- Cold chain capability: 2-8 C, down to **-80 C**
- 150 pallet spaces in freezer room
- Direct truck docking system linked to cold chamber (unbroken cold chain)
- GDP-compliant end-to-end process from docking to dispatch at constant -20 C
- Solar installation since 2018: ~1,280 MWh/year green electricity
- Uses **SwissConnect** courier network for express deliveries to pharmacies, hospitals, and medical practices
[SOURCE: https://www.alloga.ch/en/about-alloga/about-alloga.php]
[SOURCE: https://www.alloga.ch/en/logistics-solutions/warehousing-storage-area.php]
[SOURCE: https://swissconnect.ch/en/why-choose-us/success-stories/alloga-ag]

**Voigt AG** (Romanshorn, eastern Switzerland):
- Pharmaceutical wholesale and pre-wholesale
- 99% service level guaranteed
- GDP and GMP certified
- Planning 50/50 joint venture with PHOENIX Pharma Switzerland for distribution
- Less relevant to the corridor (eastern Switzerland base)
[SOURCE: https://www.voigt.ch/en/about-us/about-voigt]

### 1.3 Night Pharma Logistics

**Swiss Post "Innight" Medica**:
- Overnight logistics delivering medical products directly to **hospital operating theaters** before 6:00 AM
- Manages delivery points, anonymized keys, and badges for **220+ hospitals** across Switzerland
- Returns collected 17:00-20:00 at 220+ hospitals
- Helps hospitals reduce stock levels and capital requirements
- Covers all hospitals in Switzerland and Liechtenstein
[SOURCE: https://www.post.ch/en/sending-parcels/express-and-courier/swiss-express-innight-medica]

**SwissConnect Courier Network**:
- Network of **80+ qualified courier companies** across Switzerland
- **700+ local couriers** with various transport means

- Used by Alloga for express pharma deliveries
[SOURCE: https://swissconnect.ch/en/our-services/delivery-methods/overnight-shipping]

### 1.4 Cold Chain & Vaccines

- Swiss pharmaceutical cold chain market is significant; Geneva and Basel are critical logistics centers
- Cantonal pharmacists are **legally responsible** for vaccine logistics in each canton
- Distribution channels: mass vaccination centers, primary care physicians, mobile teams
- Real-time freight tracking already standard for vaccine cold chain
- Alloga's Burgdorf facility handles storage from +25 C to -80 C (for mRNA vaccines, biologics)
[SOURCE: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9340070/]

### 1.5 Air Freight (Geneva Airport)

- Geneva Airport (GVA) handled **90,705 tonnes of cargo in 2024** (+41.8% vs 2023)
- Air freight was 74.8% of total cargo in 2023 (47,734 tonnes)
- Export freight: 50.9%, Import freight: 49.1%
- Airport focus: luxury goods, precision instruments, perishable cargo
- Zurich (ZRH) is Switzerland's main pharma cargo hub, but Geneva handles significant volumes
- E-commerce growth contributed to 2024 cargo surge
[SOURCE: https://rapports.gva.ch/ra/2023/en/Article/poursuite-reprise]
[SOURCE: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geneva_Airport]

### 1.6 Medication Shortages

- **700+ medicines currently unavailable** in Switzerland (as of early 2026)
- Causes: fragile international supply chains, heavy dependence on Far East active ingredient production
- Galenica can ensure **99% of available medications delivered within one day**
- Galenica launched **Safety Stock Initiative** for generic medicines for chronic diseases
- Federal government developing national strategy: compulsory stocks, early-warning system, domestic production
[SOURCE: https://www.galenica.com/en/news/entries/20260113-6399860776-news.php]

## 4. MEDICAL WASTE

### 4.1 Regulatory Framework

- Medical waste in Switzerland regulated by OFEV (Federal Office for the Environment, BAFU)
- Dangerous medical waste must be **incinerated under controlled conditions**
- Some dangerous waste must be **sterilized, disinfected, or inactivated** before disposal
- Transport requires ADR/SDR-compliant vehicles and full traceability
- Enterprises must have an OMoD identification number to handle special waste
[SOURCE: https://www.bafu.admin.ch/bafu/en/home/topics/waste/guide-to-waste-a-z/medical-waste.html]
[SOURCE: https://www.ge.ch/dechets-entreprises/dechets-speciaux-professionnels-dechets-medicaux]

### 4.2 HUG Waste (Geneva)

- Hospital activities generated **5,200+ tonnes** of waste in 2024
- Breakdown: **2,365 tonnes** incinerable waste, **1,979 tonnes** recyclable
- Most recycled: paper/cardboard (633 t), food waste (403 t)
- **Medical waste specifically**: decreased from 396 tonnes (2023) to **361 tonnes** (2024)
- Waste recycling rate: **46%** in 2024
- 30+ years of sorting/recycling infrastructure
- Eco-points system since 2013
- Plastic reduction: eliminated bed covers saved 8,500 kg plastic in 2022
[SOURCE: https://www.hug.ch/en/durabilite/realisations/waste-management-and-reduction]

### 4.3 Waste Collection Companies

**Transvoirie**:
- Operates in Geneva, Vaud, Fribourg cantons
- Locations: Lancy, Thonex, Lausanne, Renens, Chatel-Saint-Denis
- Services: hospital waste collection and treatment, ADR/SDR-compliant vehicles
- Staff training for medical waste sorting
- Full traceability for infectious materials
[SOURCE: https://www.transvoirie.ch/hopitaux-collecte-traitement-et-recyclage-1185]

### 6.4 The Dead Window (01:00-05:00)

During this period:

- Hospital internal logistics continue (AGVs at CHUV run 24h)
- Swiss Post Innight trucks are en route for 06:00 delivery
- Emergency pharma requests handled by SwissConnect courier network (road only)
- **All corridor supply chains are 100% road-dependent during this window**
- Night workers at Daillens must commute by car (no train stop despite proximity to rail line)
