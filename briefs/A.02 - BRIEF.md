## A.02

# DATA SYNCHRONICITY

## Assigned 23.02 \- Due 03.03

In this assignment, you are tasked with developing a data-narrative for your analysis of City101. Think about how you can use data to reveal qualities and gaps in the city fabric, and what these could mean, especially in a linear city which is about connectivity, speed of access, and closeness.  
You will need to 1\) develop a strong an unique narrative focus, 2\) refine or combine datasets to support your analyses, and perform possible comparisons to Zurich, the main contrasting entity to City101, and 3\) begin to document the city through Point Cloud Sections, using more available online data to begin to understand the city in a different way.  
This assignment lays the foundation for your future project, so remember to orient your investigations toward the network strategy proposal, and ultimately, architectural ones.

**TASKS**

*First week*

- Based on **collective datasets** from the first assignment, brainstorm with the LLM to refine your research and develop a **narrative**.  
- The more nuanced and specific, the better.  
- This narrative can be seen as an “index” to assess the “quality” of the City at this location, according to important sets of values, for example:  
  	**ACCESSIBILITY** (Walkability, trains, horizontal elevator, etc.)  
  	**RELATIONSHIP TO LAKE** (Views, public/private, etc.)  
  	**DIVERSITY / HETEROGENEITY** (Demographics, names, etc.)  
- Refine and/or combine datasets to develop this index, based around your narrative..  
- Using your index, identify sites of **high-potential** inside City101.  
- Using your index, it can also be powerful to **compare City101 to Zurich**.

*Second week*

- Further refine your index and locations based on feedback, and begin thinking of a **networked intervention strategy**.  
- For the high-potential areas, generate Point Cloud Sections, using Federal Lidar Scans. (This is preparatory work for the midterm)

**Materials**

- Swisstopo Point-Cloud Scans (**swissSURFACE3D**)  
- Zurich Geodata (In progress, working on it :-) )

**Deliverable:**

- A Clear Narrative for your flows (why, what do they show, what do they say about the city, especially versus Zurich).  
- Updated Maps of City101 according to your narrative / index.  
- Your Formula for an **urban potential index** (which datasets, why, etc.)  
- Comparison metrics to Zurich (if interesting).  
- **Sequence of Point-Cloud Sections for locations of interest.**
