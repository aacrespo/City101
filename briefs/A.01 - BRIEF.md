In this assignment, you will begin to engage with City101 by mapping its data flows with the help of the LLM.

For this assignment, use the LLM and MCP to develop geodata mappings based on online sources (OSM, Google Places, official datasets, online sources, etc). Use Claude (or other LLMs of your choice) to gather online datasets based around your flows, then map them using basic QGIS functionalities.

Feel free to experiment and create a minimum of **two datasets per person** for next week (they can be related, or completely unrelated, and you can create as many as you like).  
These datasets will feed into the common City101 data pool for the next assignment.

**Tasks**
