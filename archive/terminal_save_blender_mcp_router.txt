❯ How can I keep my computer from turning off?                                

⏺ On Mac, go to System Settings → Displays → Advanced and turn off the
  sleep/screen off timer. Or quicker:

  caffeinate -d -i -s

  This keeps your Mac awake (display, idle sleep, and system sleep all
  prevented). It runs until you Ctrl+C it.

  If you want it to last a specific duration (e.g. 12 hours):

  caffeinate -d -i -s -t 43200

  Run it in a separate terminal window and leave it open overnight.
